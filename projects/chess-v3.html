Chess [v3]

Created May 2025

Chess [v3] (repo) is my third iteration on a chess bot. Previously, I made v1 which just randomly selected valid moves and v2 which had a one-ply look ahead that made the bot super greedy -- it would eat any piece it saw and generally just didn't care about its pieces getting captured.

v3 now utilizes a state of the art 3-ply minmax algorithm to select moves to crush opponents. 3-ply implies that the bot looks ahead for 3 half-moves, which means it can more accurately assess captures. Notably, this means the bot is not as greedy as before.

Anyways, some notable features of this chess bot are that it:

  • Can play chess.
  • Can sometimes make some super good moves.
  • Can spot all mate in 1s.
  • Can usually win most games when the opponent resigns.
  • Has beaten my little brother at chess.

Some other notable features of my bot are that it:

  • Gives lots of checks. I've biased my algorithm to do this so hopfeully one of these checks becomes checkmate.
  • Will take a piece if it attacks it twice. This is a feature of the 3-ply algorithm; the bot calculates that if it takes then you take then it takes, it'll be up material, not realizing that you might be able to take back again.
  • Uses a fixed algorithm. This makes it kinda predictable :/.
  • Sometimes breaks. En passant is weird I guess.
  • Does not implement draw offers. And can't detect 3-fold repetition or insufficient material situations. In other words, it either wins or loses (or gets stalemated).
